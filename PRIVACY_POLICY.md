
# Privacy Policy for Indigo Habits

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Indigo Habits ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Journal Entries**: Text, photos, and audio recordings you create
- **Habits**: Custom habits you create and track
- **Affirmations**: Custom affirmations you create
- **Profile Information**: Optional name and profile picture

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (features used, time spent)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information when the app crashes

### Information from Third Parties

- **RevenueCat**: Subscription status and purchase history
- **Apple/Google**: Account information if you sign in with Apple/Google

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Save your journal entries, habits, and affirmations
- Process subscription payments via RevenueCat
- Send reminder notifications (if enabled)
- Improve the app based on usage patterns
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All your journal entries, habits, and affirmations are stored locally on your device using SQLite
- **Cloud Backup**: We do not automatically backup your data to the cloud
- **Subscription Data**: RevenueCat stores your subscription status

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We only share data with:

- **RevenueCat**: For subscription management and payment processing
- **Apple/Google**: For in-app purchase processing
- **Analytics Services**: Anonymized usage data for app improvement (if implemented)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Delete**: Clear all your data from the app (Settings â†’ Clear All Data)
- **Export**: Export your journal entries as PDF
- **Opt-Out**: Disable notifications and analytics

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

- Local encryption for sensitive data
- Secure communication with payment processors
- No transmission of journal content to external servers

## Children's Privacy

Indigo Habits is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## In-App Purchases

- Subscription: $4.40/month for Premium features
- Processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
- Managed by RevenueCat
- Cancel anytime through your App Store/Play Store account

## Notifications

If you enable notifications:

- We send local reminders for habits and journaling
- No data is sent to external servers
- You can disable notifications in Settings

## Changes to This Policy
