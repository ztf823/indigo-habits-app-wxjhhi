## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### Building Habits
1. **Start small**: Begin with just 1-2 habits
2. **Be specific**: "Exercise for 10 minutes" vs "Exercise"
3. **Stack habits**: Do new habits after existing routines
4. **Track daily**: Open the app every day, even if just to check in
5. **Celebrate wins**: Enjoy the green glow and haptic feedback!

### Journaling
1. **Write freely**: No judgment, just express yourself
2. **Add photos**: Visual memories make entries more meaningful
3. **Be consistent**: Even a few sentences daily builds the habit
4. **Review progress**: Look back at your calendar to see growth
5. **Use affirmations**: Let them guide your mindset for the day

### Affirmations
1. **Read daily**: Start your day with a positive affirmation
2. **Personalize**: Create custom affirmations that resonate with you
3. **Believe**: Affirmations work when you truly embrace them
4. **Vary them**: Generate new ones to keep things fresh
5. **Save favorites**: (Coming soon!) Keep the ones that inspire you most

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### App not loading?
- Check your internet connection (for first launch)
- Restart the app
- Clear app cache if needed

### Affirmations not generating?
- You may have used your 5 free affirmations
- Upgrade to Pro for unlimited
- Or create custom affirmations (always free!)

### Habits not saving?
- Make sure you have storage permissions
- Check device storage space
- Data is saved locally, so it persists offline

### Photos not attaching?
- Grant camera/photo library permissions
- Check storage space on device
- Try selecting a different photo
